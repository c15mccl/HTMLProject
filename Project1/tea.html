Iced Green Tea


An iced green tea is a simple, green tea
(contains caffeine) with water or
lemonade (contains caffeine), and ice.

Iced Black Tea


An iced black tea is a simple, black tea
(contains caffeine) with water or
lemonade(contains caffeine), and ice.

Iced White Tea


An iced white tea is a simple, white tea
(contains caffeine) with water or
lemonade (contains caffeine), and ice.

Iced Passion Tea


An iced passion tea is a simple,
passion tea (contains NO caffeine) with
water or lemonade (contains caffeine), and ice.

Iced Peach Green Tea


An iced peach green tea is a simple,
green tea (contains caffeine) with
water or lemonade (contains caffeine),
peach juice, and ice.

Iced Guava White Tea


An iced guava white tea is a simple,
white tea (contains caffeine) with
water or lemonade (contains caffeine),
guava juice, and ice.
