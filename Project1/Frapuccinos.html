Coffee Frappuccino


A coffee frappuccino is a blended, sweetened coffee beverage.

Mocha Frappuccino


A mocha frappuccino is a blended, sweetened,
coffee beverage flavored with mocha syrup (chocolate),
and topped with whipped cream (optional).

Caramel Frappuccino


A caramel frappuccino is a blended, sweetened,
coffee beverage flavored with caramel syrup,
and topped with whipped cream (optional).

Vanilla Bean Frappuccino


A vanilla bean frappuccino is a blended,
sweetened, cream-based beverage (does not contain coffee)
flavored with vanilla bean powder, and topped with whipped
cream (optional).

Stawberries and Cream Frappuccino


A strawberries and cream frappuccino
is a blended, sweetened, cream-based beverage
(does not contain coffee) flavored with
strawberry puree, and topped with whipped
cream (optional).
